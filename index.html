ARMA
AUGMENTED REALITY MUSIC ACT
ARMA technology with the help of neural networks recognizes movements and allows you to extract sound literally from the air. There are invisible instruments, notes and chords, around the user, hits on which the camera of a smartphone recognizes and instantly turns into the music.
Place the device above waist at a distance of two meters
Select mode
Mode ‘Flow’
Hit the keys with your left hand. Model sound with your right hand.
Mode ‘Guitar’
Move your left hand horizontally to hold the accord. Hit with your right hand to play.
